# 🪨 STRATICA CODE OF CONDUCT

## Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct v2.1

### Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

---

### Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

* 🧠 **Demonstrating empathy and kindness** toward other people
* 🪨 **Respecting different opinions, viewpoints, and experiences**
* 🤝 **Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback**
* 🌍 **Prioritizing ethical research practices**
* 🔬 **Acknowledging Indigenous knowledge and traditional geological knowledge**

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

* ❌ The use of sexualized language or imagery
* ❌ Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
* ❌ Public or private harassment
* ❌ Publishing others' private information without permission
* ❌ Data fabrication or manipulation
* ❌ Disrespecting fossil and core sample handling ethics

---

### Special Considerations for STRATICA Research

As this project involves geological and paleoclimate research:

* 🪨 **Respecting sample integrity** - All core samples must be handled with proper protocols
* 🔐 **Respecting Indigenous knowledge** when studying traditional land and fossil knowledge
* 📋 **Obtaining proper permits** for any field research involving sample collection
* 🏷️ **Proper attribution** of all data sources and contributions
* 🌎 **Acknowledging geopolitical boundaries** when working with international drill cores
